Bacon Cheeseburger

This bacon cheeseburger is just waiting to be devoured!

With crispy bacon, melted chedder, and our special sauce this mouthwatering concoction can fill the belly of even the mightiest of men.

Go ahead and give it a shot. If you don't like it, I don't think anything can save you.

Ingredients:

  • Sesame seed buns
  • Ground beef
  • Bacon
  • Chedder cheese
  • mayonnaise
  • Ketchup
  • Relish
  • Dill pickles
  • Red onions
  • Lettuce
  • Tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Cut buns
  2. Fry ground beef into patties
  3. Fry bacon until cooked to desired crispness
  4. Combine mayonnaise, ketchup, and relish
  5. Layer each burger patty with chedder cheese and top with four slices of bacon
  6. Spread the mixture we just made onto the top of each bun
  7. Finally top with pickles, onions, lettuce, and tomatoes
