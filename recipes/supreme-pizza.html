Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough
  • Red sauce
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Pepperoni
  • Italian sausage
  • Salami
  • Canadian bacon
  • Onion
  • Green peppers
  • Black olives
  • Mushrooms
  • Tomatoes

Steps:

  1. Roll pizza dough into an 18" circle
  2. Spray pizza pan with cooking oil and lay flattened dough across
  3. Go around the edge and form a crust
  4. Liberally spoon red sauce until the dough in covered
  5. Layer the mozzarella cheese on top
  6. Next pile on the four types of meat listed in the ingredient section
  7. Finally top with remaining vegetables
  8. Bake at 550° for twenty-two minutes or until golden brown on top
